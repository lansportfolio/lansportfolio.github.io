How do I make my website crawlable

Published: 2025-11-01

  2. 2. Verify Ownership

I've been using my Hexo website for years as a personal journal - it's my favorite space where I could write freely without worrying about whether anyone could actually find it. But recently, as I decided to take a step toward running my own online shop, I realized it was time to change that.

If I want people to discover my work, then it's essential to make sure Google can actually find my website.

The overall process is quite straightforward. Here are the general steps most website owners follow to get their site indexed:

1. Add Your Site to Google Search Console

The first step is to let Google know your site exists.

  • Go to Google Search Console.

  • Click ‚ÄúAdd property‚Äù.

  • Select ‚ÄúURL prefix‚Äù.

  • Enter your full site URL (e.g., https://yoursite.github.io/).

This step essentially registers your website with Google, opening the door for indexing and search visibility.

2. Verify Ownership

Next, Google needs to confirm that you actually own the site before indexing it. There are several verification methods:

  • HTML file: Upload a verification file to your Hexo site.
  • HTML tag: Add a verification meta tag to your site's <head> section.
  • Google Analytics: Use an existing GA property to verify ownership.

3. Submit Your Sitemap

A sitemap gives Google a clear structure of your website, making the indexing process faster and more efficient.

Although submitting a sitemap is optional for websites with fewer than 500 pages, it's still a best practice.

  • Generate a sitemap for your Hexo site.
  • Go to Sitemaps in GSC and enter your sitemap URL (e.g., https://yoursite.com/sitemap.xml).

That's the typical GSC submission workflow. But since I also wanted to monitor my website traffic through Google Analytics, I flipped the order a bit:

This approach made the process more streamlined and ensured that both indexing and traffic tracking were set up at the same time.

So, here's the exact process I followed to get my Hexo website indexed:

  1. Open Google Search.
  2. Enter your site URL in search bar, eg. site:yoursite.github.io.

Let's move on.

Step 2: Create your organization

  1. Go to Google Marketing Platform.
  2. Click "Admin management".
  3. Click "Create Organization".
  4. Enter your organization name.

Step 3: Create your account

  1. Enter your account name.

Step 4: Create your property

  1. Enter your property name.

Step 5: Create your business

  1. Enter your business name.
  2. Select your business size.

Step 6: Choose your business objectives

  1. Select anything fits your goals.

Step 7: Set up your data stream

  1. Select "Web".
  2. Enter your site URL.
  3. Pick up a stream name.

Step 8: Set up a google tag
